About Us

Connecting Rwanda's Education Community

Education Updates Rwanda – Kivu is a community-driven platform dedicated to sharing the latest education news, scholarships, resources, and inspiring stories across Rwanda and the Great Lakes region.

Our Purpose

Mission, Vision & Goal

01

Our Mission

To empower Rwandan students, educators, and families with timely, accurate, and actionable education information that drives better outcomes and expanded opportunities.

02

Our Vision

A Rwanda where every student — regardless of location or background — has access to quality education information, opportunities, and the inspiration to achieve their full potential.

03

Our Goal

To become the most trusted and widely-used education information platform across the Great Lakes region, bridging the information gap for every student and educator.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

🎯

Accuracy

We verify all information before publishing so students and families can trust every update we share.

⚡

Timeliness

Education news moves fast — we ensure critical deadlines, results, and policies reach you instantly.

🤝

Inclusivity

We serve all Rwandans — rural and urban, primary to university — ensuring no student is left behind.

💡

Empowerment

Every piece of content we produce is designed to give students the knowledge and confidence to succeed.

Our Journey

Organisation History

2020

YouTube Channel Founded

Education Updates Rwanda – Kivu launched as a YouTube channel to share education news and scholarship opportunities for Rwandan students.

2021

10,000 Subscribers Milestone

Reached 10,000 YouTube subscribers, confirming the platform's value to Rwanda's education community.

2022

Scholarship Database Launched

Launched a dedicated scholarship tracking system, helping thousands of students find and apply for opportunities.

2023

All 5 Provinces Covered

Expanded coverage to all five provinces of Rwanda with local reporters and community contributors in each region.

2024

Full Website Platform

Launched this comprehensive web platform combining news, videos, resources, gallery, and scholarship tracking in one place.

2025

680 Schools Covered

Our network now covers 680 schools nationwide with real-time updates, exam news, and student support resources.
